Virat Kohli Achievements

National Honours
  • Arjuna Award (2013): A recognition of Kohli's emergence as a sporting icon in India.
  • Padma Shri (2017): Bestowed for his exceptional contribution to Indian cricket, reflecting his status as a national hero.
  • Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award (2018): India’s highest sporting honour, acknowledging Kohli's unparalleled achievements in cricket.
Sporting Honours
  • A Stand Named in His Honor (2019): The Arun Jaitley Stadium in Delhi paying homage to Kohli by renaming a stand after him, a rare honour for an active player.
  • IPL Orange Cap: For scoring the maximum runs (973) in IPL 2016, Kohli was provided with the Orange Cup and he also won the Most Valuable Player award that season.
Virat Kohli ICC Awards
  • Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (2011–2020): Recognizing Kohli as the ICC Men's Cricketer of the Decade, a period marked by his dominance in all formats.
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018): Aproof of Kohli's consistency and excellence at the international level.
  • ICC Men's ODI Cricketer of the Decade (2011–2020): Celebrating his sustained brilliance in one-day internationals.
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year (2012, 2017, 2018): Acknowledging his remarkable performances and match-winning abilities.
  • ICC Test Player of the Year (2018): An accolade that highlights Kohli's mastery in the longest format of the game.
  • ICC ODI Team of the Year (2012, 2014, 2016–2019): Featuring as a captain in most instances, showcasing his leadership skills.
  • ICC Test Team of the Year (2017–2019): Leading the team as a captain, a reflection of his strategic acumen.
  • ICC Men's T20I Team of the Year (2022): A nod to his adaptability across formats.
  • ICC Spirit of Cricket (2019): Recognizing his exemplary conduct on the field, during World Cup 2023 when he urged the crowd to applaud Steve Smith and not boo him.
  • ICC ODI Cricketer of the Year (2023): For his astonishing performance in the year 2023 and scoring 765 runs in the World Cup, Kohli was awarded the ICC ODI Player of the Year.
